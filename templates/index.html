Internal Confluence Chatbot

This is an exploratory use of ChatGPT on our internal Confluence documentation. Basically, we modify in the background your question to add some context.

The context is that of a Confluence note that appears to be the most similar to the question. Similarity in Natural Language Processing (NLP) is resolved by converting a text into a vector, also called embeddings, and a simple vector similarity metric is used to identify texts that are similar.

Once the most similar document is found, its content is added to the prompt. ChatGPT uses then the context to answer the question.

Although not perfect, the tool can provide a more useful choice for the document (or documents) to look at to answer your question with more depth.
